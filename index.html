Gameplay

Welcome to the infinite universe where you must repair and upgrade your spaceship. And not only that! You have to defend yourself against enemy raids or even prepare your cannons for meteorites. Colonize new planets, add them to your colony and smash all the enemies. Travel through this dangerous universe. You never know what's coming.

Programing engine

Endless Space is programmed and developed in Turbowarp (compiles scratch projects into JavaScript, adding new capabilities and enabling high performance), and yes scratch is a platform for teaching young children about programming - but that's the challenge, because these platforms are not as flexible as, say, unity.

Textures

Some of the textures are from the internet, some I painted myself. Since I'm not a graphic designer, I would be happy if someone would contact me and offer textures. Since the project is not funded in any way, don't expect money in return.
